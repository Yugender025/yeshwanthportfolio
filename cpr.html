CPR

THE PROBLEM

Many people surveyed during our initial research phase agreed that CPR would be a useful life skill to have. However, most people expressed a feeling of uncertainty about where to begin their learning journey.

We conducted some initial research, developed an interview questionnaire, and surveyed multiple participants. Going into our focus groups, we weren’t sure which components participants would find most useful, but kept an open mind. We discovered that users liked that they could use the app to learn some CPR basics, but also had the option to pursue accreditation. ​

The feedback we received made it clear that participants wanted to use the app for a variety of cases. We used this feedback to begin iterating on designs.
