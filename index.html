Movie ticketing app

The problem for a mobile ticketing app for a movie theater is that customers often face long lines at the theater, especially during peak hours, to purchase tickets and are confused about the seating layout. This can be frustrating for customers looking to buy tickets quickly and efficiently.

My Role

I was the UX designer for movie ticketing app.

Main goal

The goal of mobile ticketing app for movie theater is to provide customers with a seamless and convenient way to purchase tickets.
