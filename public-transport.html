Public Transport

THE PROBLEM

People are difficult to find exact timings of buses from their bus stops, and bus locations of the buses travelling.

To better understand our target audience as their requirement we performed user research on public transport people who using daily. When we reach out public we understand their problems and their difficulties while waiting for buses in their bus stops, the don’t have any idea in bus timings, unable find the current location of buses.​

Moreover, we discovered that customers want the opportunity to see current location of buses and share location with respective users, so users are easily can identified which route buses are travelling. Overall, the user research assisted us in better understanding the requirements and preferences of our customers.
